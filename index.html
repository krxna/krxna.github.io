ABOUT ME

I've always been fascinated by two seemingly different worlds: the elegant logic of machines and the beautiful complexity of human beings. My journey in Computer Science is my attempt to build a bridge between them.

On the other, I'm into exploring human behavior and biology to understand the 'why' behind our actions and emotions.

I believe the most impactful technology is not just smart, but also empathetic. My goal is to create solutions that are technically robust and designed with a deep understanding of the people they are meant to serve.
